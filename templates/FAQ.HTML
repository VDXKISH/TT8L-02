Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

  • Q: How do I create an account?

    A: To create an account, click on the "Login" button, fill in the required details, and follow the instructions to complete the registration process.

Booking and Tickets

  • Q: How do I book a bus ticket?

    A: To book a ticket, enter your departure and destination locations, select your travel date, choose from the available buses, and follow the prompts to complete the booking process.

  • Q: Can I book tickets for multiple passengers?

    A: Yes, you can book tickets for multiple passengers in a single transaction. Just enter the details of each passenger when prompted during the booking process.

Payment and pricing

  • Q: What payment methods are accepted?

    A: We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, digital wallets, and online banking.

  • Q: Are there any discounts available?

    A: Children (4 to 12 years old): MYR 12, Adults (13 to 59 years old): MYR 22, Senior Citizens (60 years and above): Free

Travel Information

  • Q: What documents do I need to carry while traveling?

    A: You should carry a printed or digital copy of your ticket and a valid government-issued ID. Some operators might have specific requirements, so please check the details on your ticket.

  • Q: How early should I arrive at the bus station?

    A: We recommend arriving at least 30 minutes before the scheduled departure time to ensure a smooth boarding process.

Additional Services

  • Q: Can I choose snacks for my bus ride?

    A: Yes, you can select snacks for your bus ride during the booking process. You will be given the option to choose from a variety of snacks.

  • Q: How do I select snacks for my trip?

    A: During the booking process, after selecting your seat, you will be prompted to choose from a list of available snacks.

  • Q: What types of snacks are available?

    A: The types of snacks are available is Sandwich, Chicken Burgee, 7Days Vanilla Croissant, 7Days Chocolate Croissant, Nasi Lemak, and Chicken Rice

  • Q: Can I bring my own snacks?

    A: While we provide a variety of snacks for your convenience, you are welcome to bring your own snacks if you prefer.
